GE 10A(2018)-X (LEC) WPE (Whole Person Education)

A project crafted to explore the sacred dimensions of faith as experienced in the Church, Classroom, Court, Cultural, and Community settings.

"For now we see in a mirror dimly, but then face to face. Now I know in part; then I shall know fully, even as I have been fully known." - 1 Corinthians 13:12

About the Author

Rod Troy N. Gaudan

I am someone who wants to learn more about truth, faith, and how they connect to everyday life. I am a BSIT student, and I made this project for the subject GE 10 (Living in the IT Era) to share my spiritual journey.

This project shows my visit to five special places where faith is practiced, tested, and celebrated in different ways.

Location Dumaguete City, Negros Oriental, Philippines
Institution Silliman University
Course Bachelor of Science in Information Technology
